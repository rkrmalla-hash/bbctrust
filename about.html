About BBC Trust

Dedicated to supporting communities through education, healthcare, social welfare and charitable service initiatives across Andhra Pradesh.

Who We Are

BBC Trust is a charitable organization committed to improving the lives of individuals and communities through meaningful social service activities.

Serving Society With Compassion

BBC Trust works towards community development by supporting education, healthcare services, awareness programs and social welfare initiatives.

Our goal is to create a positive social impact by helping underprivileged families, students and communities through charitable activities and support programs.

We believe that collective support and community participation can help build a healthier, educated and empowered society.

Our Vision & Mission

Building stronger communities through service, support and social responsibility.

Our Vision

To create a compassionate society where every individual has access to education, healthcare and community support.

Our Mission

To conduct impactful charitable programs that support communities, families and individuals in need across Andhra Pradesh.

Community Support

We focus on educational support, healthcare awareness, food assistance and social welfare initiatives for community development.
