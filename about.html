About Bapu Bhushanum Charitable Trust

Bapu Bhushanum Charitable Trust is a registered charitable trust in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, dedicated to helping poor and needy people through social welfare activities, Annadanam programs, and community support services since 2010.

Serving Society With Care and Compassion

Bapu Bhushanum Charitable Trust was established with the vision of helping people and supporting communities through charitable activities and social service programs. Since 2010, our trust has been working to provide assistance to individuals and families who need support during difficult situations.

Our trust actively participates in Annadanam programs, social welfare activities, community support initiatives, and public service efforts. We believe that even a small act of kindness can make a meaningful difference in someone's life and help build a stronger society.

Through the support of volunteers, donors, and well-wishers, we continue our efforts to help poor families, elderly people, students, and individuals facing hardships. Our activities are focused on providing support, encouragement, and hope to those who need assistance.

As a charitable trust in Visakhapatnam, we are committed to serving society with honesty, responsibility, and compassion. We work towards creating positive change through community welfare activities and encourage people to participate in charitable and social service programs.

Our mission is to continue expanding our welfare activities, reach more beneficiaries, and contribute towards building a caring and supportive community across Visakhapatnam and Andhra Pradesh.
