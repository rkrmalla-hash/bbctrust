Welcome To BBC Trust

Bapu Bhushanam Charitable Trust works towards creating positive social impact through charitable activities and community welfare programs.

Empowering Society With Compassion

Our trust supports underprivileged communities through food donation, educational assistance, healthcare awareness and social welfare activities.

We believe in helping families, children and needy individuals gain access to opportunities, support and essential services.

Through teamwork and public participation, BBC Trust continues to work towards meaningful and sustainable community development.
