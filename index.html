About Our Trust

Bapu Bhushanum Charitable Trust is committed to serving poor and needy communities through compassion, social service and welfare initiatives.

Helping Society With Compassion

Established in 2010, our trust works towards supporting underprivileged communities through Annadanam, orphan support and social welfare activities.

We believe that service to humanity is the highest form of spirituality and social responsibility.

With public support and volunteers, our trust continues creating positive change in society.

Founders Message

Malla Ramakrishna Rao & Family

Founders - Bapu Bhushanum Charitable Trust

Our mission is to serve society through compassion, humanity and charitable support activities. We strongly believe that every helping hand can bring hope and support to needy communities.

Through Annadanam, orphan support and welfare initiatives, our trust continuously works towards creating positive social impact and supporting underprivileged people across society.

With dedication, spirituality and public support, we aim to inspire kindness and build a stronger and more caring community for future generations.
