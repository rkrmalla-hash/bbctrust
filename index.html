Welcome To BBC Trust

Bapu Bhushanam Charitable Trust works towards creating a better society through charitable activities, community support, food distribution, orphan support and welfare programs.

Empowering Lives With Compassion

Our trust supports poor families, children, elderly people and underprivileged communities through social welfare activities and charitable support programs.

We conduct food distribution programs, educational assistance, orphan support and healthcare awareness activities across Visakhapatnam.

Together with public support and volunteers, BBC Trust continues working towards meaningful community development and positive social impact.
