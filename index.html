Welcome To BBC Trust

BBC Trust (Bapu Bhushanam Charitable Trust) works towards creating positive social impact through charitable activities and community support programs.

Empowering Society With Compassion

Our trust supports underprivileged communities through educational support, healthcare awareness programs, social welfare initiatives and community development activities.

We believe in creating a stronger society by helping individuals and families gain access to opportunities, support and essential services.

Through teamwork and public participation, BBC Trust continues to work towards meaningful and sustainable community impact.

Our Key Initiatives

Supporting communities through impactful charitable and welfare programs.

Educational Support

Providing educational assistance, awareness programs and learning support for students.

Healthcare Services

Organizing health camps, medical awareness programs and healthcare support initiatives.

Social Welfare

Supporting families and individuals through charitable and community welfare activities.

Community Development

Encouraging sustainable community growth through social responsibility initiatives.
